Mac and Cheese

A creamy, cheesy comfort dish made with tender pasta coated in a rich, buttery cheese sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups macroni
  • 2 cups shredded cheese
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • salt &pepper

Directions

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Make the sauce base
  3. Add flour
  4. Add milk.
  5. Thicken
  6. Add cheese
  7. Combine pasta and sauce
  8. Serve hot!!

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
