Egg Fried Rice

Egg fried rice is a quick, comforting dish made with rice, friend egg, adn simple seasong. I chose it as my favourite because it's easy to make, always tasty, and reminds me of home-style comfort.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1-2 eggs
  • 1-2 tbps oil (vegetable or olive oil)
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1-2 tbps soy sauce
  • salt &pepper
  • Green onions
  • Chilli flakes or sriacha

Directions

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add garlic and stir for a few seconds.
  3. Add rice, soy sauce, salt, adn pepper.
  4. In another pan, fry an egg untill white is set.
  5. Place rice on a plate and put the fried egg on top.
  6. Garnish with green onions or chili flaskes.
