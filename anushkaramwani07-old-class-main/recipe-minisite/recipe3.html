Spagetti

A simple Italian pasta tossed in garlic-infused olive oil with a hint of chili for flavor and warmth.

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 1-3 tbps olive oil
  • 3-4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • Â½ tsp red chili flakes (optional for spice)
  • salt &pepper
  • Fresh Parsley

Directions

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Heat oil
  3. Cook garlic.
  4. Add chili flakes
  5. Add pasta
  6. Add Pasta water
  7. Season
  8. Serve hot!

Prep Time: 10-12 minutes
Cook Time: 15-17 minutes
